## What it is
AI-powered video creation platform that turns text prompts into fully edited videos. Integrates OpenAI Sora 2 and Google VEO 3.1 for AI video generation. Designed for rapid short-form content production -- the source video demonstrates creating 20 YouTube Shorts in 20 minutes.

## How I can use it
- Batch-create short-form video content (YouTube Shorts, Reels, TikToks) from text prompts
- Generate videos with AI voiceover, stock footage, and captions without manual editing
- Clone your voice for consistent narration across videos

## Pricing
- **Free**: 10 AI minutes/week, watermarked exports, no commercial rights
- **Plus** ($28/mo): 50 AI minutes/month, 80 iStock credits, 2 voice clones, watermark-free, commercial rights
- **Max** ($50-60/mo): 200 AI minutes/month, 320 iStock credits, 5 voice clones
- Annual billing saves ~20%. Unused minutes do not roll over.
